Twitch Emote Size Requirements

To ensure your emotes look great on all devices, Twitch requires three specific sizes for every upload:

  • 112x112 px: The high-resolution master used for larger displays and retina screens.
  • 56x56 px: The medium size used in standard chat views.
  • 28x28 px: The small size used in dense chat environments.

All emotes must be in PNG format with a transparent background and a file size under 1MB.

How to Resize Emotes for Twitch

  1. Select your artwork: Choose a high-quality PNG or JPEG image. Square images work best.
  2. Drag and Drop: Simply drop your file into our secure resizer above.
  3. Instant Generation: Our tool instantly creates all three required Twitch sizes.
  4. Download: Click 'Download' for each size to save them to your device, ready for upload to your Twitch dashboard.
